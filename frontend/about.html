About FarmBuddy 🌿

We are on a mission to build a direct, transparent, and fair bridge between India's farmers and every home that values fresh, healthy food.

Our Story

FarmBuddy was born out of a simple idea: farmers deserve better, and families deserve fresher. We saw firsthand how middlemen were eating into farmers' profits while pushing up prices for buyers.

Founded in 2022, FarmBuddy connects verified farmers directly with customers. Every product you buy on FarmBuddy is harvested and dispatched within 24 hours — no cold storage, no preservatives, no compromise.

Today we work with 120+ farmers across 15 Indian states, delivering freshness to over 10,000 families every month.

What We Stand For

Our core values guide everything we do — from farmer onboarding to your doorstep delivery.

🌿

100% Organic

We verify every farmer's practices. No harmful pesticides, no GMOs — just nature doing its best work.

🤝

Fair to Farmers

Farmers earn 3x more with us than through traditional markets. Their prosperity is our purpose.

🚚

Speed & Freshness

Harvested today, at your door tomorrow. Our logistics ensure peak freshness on every order.

🔒

Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees. You see exactly what the farmer earns and what you pay. Honest, always.

♻️

Eco Friendly

All packaging is biodegradable. We're committed to zero-waste delivery by 2025.

🏘️

Community First

We invest 5% of profits back into rural farming communities for education and infrastructure.
